Welcome!

I’m lecturer at Department of Statistics at Federal University of Paraná (UFPR, 2010 - today). At UFPR I teach Computational Statistics regularly to the Bachelor in Statistics and several topics in the Data Science & Big Data Specialization. I also teach elementary statistics and/or applied statistics, related to the data analysis, frequently.

I did my doctor degree in Statistics and Agricultural Experimentation at UFLA (2009 - 2013). Also there I did my master degree in the same program (2008 - 2009). I’am bachelor in Agronomy at UFGD (2003 - 2007).

My major competencies are in design and analysis of experiments, nonlinear regression and regression models for count data. I like thigs related to data visualization and web scraping. Recently, I’m putting some attention on text mining and machine learning.

I'm tutor of the Tutorial Education Program on the Bachelor in Statistics (2015 - today). The program provides scholarship for 12 students that develop activities on Education, Research and Extension. With these remarkable students we acomplished lots of things.

I'm co-organizer of the Data Science Curitiba MeetUp. Our mettings are to discuss tendencies, technologies and case studies from an applied and academic point of view.

I’m a R programmer. Because of that, I have experience with R language and I give short courses about R almost every semester.
